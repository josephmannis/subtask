# subtask
Recursive tasks with no limit!

## Development
1. Download the Expo app to your device and sign in.
2. Clone the repository.
3. `expo install`
4. `yarn start`

## Previewing

To download the app to your device without cloning the repository:
1. Download the Expo app to your device.
2. [Go here and scan the QR code](https://expo.io/@miniprince/projects/subtask)

Otherwise, view the mockups [here](https://www.figma.com/file/5xo0KabgbABvUcHaz8In7Q/Subtask?node-id=0%3A1)
